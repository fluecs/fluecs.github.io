Ilia's Creative and Intellectual Pursuits

Capturing Moments: Photography and Videography

Photography and videography have become much more than just hobbies for me—they are ways to freeze time and tell stories visually. I love exploring different environments, from urban streets bustling with life to quiet natural landscapes, each offering unique challenges and creative opportunities. Experimenting with lighting, composition, and camera settings lets me capture scenes exactly as I envision them or, sometimes, in ways I never expected. Videography adds another layer of complexity and excitement: framing motion, working with sound, and telling narratives that unfold second by second. Whether it's setting up a time-lapse of the night sky or creating a short documentary-style video, the process of planning, shooting, and editing is as fulfilling as seeing the final result.

  • Exploring new locations for unique shots
  • Experimenting with lenses, filters, and color grading
  • Editing footage and crafting story-driven videos

Code as a Creative Tool: Programming and Game Development

Programming allows me to build ideas from scratch and bring abstract concepts to life. I enjoy working on system-level projects where I can control how everything functions down to the smallest detail. Operating systems, tools, and utilities give me an endless playground for problem-solving and optimization. Game development, in particular, combines my love for programming with creativity and design. Creating game mechanics, designing logic systems, and building custom engines or frameworks gives me the freedom to experiment with gameplay ideas and interactive experiences. The debugging process, though often frustrating, feels like solving intricate puzzles, and watching a working build after hours of coding never gets old.

  • Developing low-level software and operating systems
  • Designing game mechanics and gameplay loops
  • Writing custom tools and game engines
  • Debugging complex logic and optimizing performance

Understanding Reality: The Fascination with Physics

Physics is where curiosity meets logic. I’ve always been fascinated by the fundamental rules that govern the universe. Whether it’s thinking about classical mechanics, thermodynamics, or delving into more abstract areas like quantum physics and relativity, I enjoy breaking down complex concepts and seeing how they connect. Often, my understanding of physics also influences my programming and game development work—for example, when simulating real-world mechanics or designing realistic in-game physics systems. Reading research papers, watching lecture series, and working through derivations in my free time keeps this passion alive and helps me see the world through a more analytical lens.

  1. Studying theoretical physics concepts in detail
  2. Applying physics principles in game development
  3. Running thought experiments and solving physics problems for fun

Where It All Comes Together

While each hobby stands on its own, they often intersect in interesting ways. My programming knowledge helps me automate editing workflows for photography and videography. My understanding of physics makes my game mechanics more realistic and grounded. Storytelling techniques from videography influence how I design user experiences in games. These crossovers not only make each hobby more enjoyable but also allow me to build projects that combine visual creativity, technical skill, and scientific curiosity—all driven by a desire to create and understand.
